  <what-built>
Automated axe-core CI gate (Tasks 1-2) covers ~57% of WCAG violations. The remaining coverage requires manual verification with a real keyboard and screen reader. This checkpoint covers the five primary keyboard flows and NVDA verification required by Phase 23 success criteria.
  </what-built>
  <how-to-verify>
**Keyboard-only walkthrough (Tab, Enter, Escape, Space — no mouse):**

1. **Auth flow:** Navigate to / with Tab. Tab to "Get Started" or login link, press Enter. On /login, Tab through email and password fields, type credentials, Tab to submit button, press Enter. Verify focus moves to /dashboard h1 after redirect.

2. **Challenge board:** On /dashboard, Tab to "Challenges" nav link, press Enter. On /challenges, Tab through challenge cards. Press Enter on one. On challenge detail, verify focus on h1. Tab to back link/button, press Enter. Verify focus returns to challenge feed.

3. **Circle workspace:** Tab to "Circles" nav link, press Enter. Tab through circle list. Press Enter on a circle. In workspace, Tab to "Form Circle" or modal trigger, press Enter. Verify modal traps focus (Tab cycles within modal). Press Escape. Verify focus returns to trigger button.

4. **Wellbeing check-in:** Tab to "Wellbeing" nav link, press Enter. On /wellbeing/checkin, Tab through the form questions. Select answers. Tab to submit. Press Enter. Verify completion state.

5. **Notification centre:** Tab to notification bell button in navbar. Press Enter or Space. Verify dropdown opens (aria-expanded="true"). Tab through notification items. Press Escape. Verify dropdown closes and focus returns to bell button.

**NVDA spot checks (if available):**
- Press H to navigate headings on each page — all announced in logical order
- Press D to navigate landmarks — main, banner, navigation present
- Open a modal — announced as "dialog" with title
- Change notification count — live region announces new count
- Navigate to skeleton loading state — announced as "Loading"

**Pass criteria:** All 5 keyboard flows complete without getting stuck or losing focus. No element is unreachable by keyboard. Focus never disappears into the void.
  </how-to-verify>
